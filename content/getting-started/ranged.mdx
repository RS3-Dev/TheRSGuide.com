---
title: Ranged Combat
description: Overview of Ranged Combat
---

Ranged is one of the 3 core styles. It can offer solid damage, but lacks utility and convenience compared to the other styles especially at the endgame. It's seen as a high skill ceiling combat style that requires intimate knowledge of the combat system to master - but can offer very high damage when executed correctly.
