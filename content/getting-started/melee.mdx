---
title: Melee Combat
description: Overview of Melee Combat
---

Melee is one of the 3 core styles. It is one of the most perilous, but rewarding combat styles to use, and is one of the most enjoyable and popular styles in RuneScape. It relies on high-hitting abilities, a wide array of special attacks, and situational use of both dual wield and 2h weapons.
