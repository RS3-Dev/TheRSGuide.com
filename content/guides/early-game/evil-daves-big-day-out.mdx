---
title: Evil Dave's Big Day Out
description: Early Herblore and Magic XP with powerful spicy stew boosts
---

Evil Dave's Big Day Out provides a lot of early game Herblore and Magic experience, and unlocks +6 skill boosts from spicy stews. These become one of the strongest temporary skill boosts, especially for Crafting and Herblore requirements.

> Tip: To maintain a 6 level boost indefinitely, exit to the lobby 55 seconds after logging into a world so your boosted stats never drain.
