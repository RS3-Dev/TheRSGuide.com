---
title: Desert Treasure
description: Unlock Ancient Magicks - the most powerful magic spellbook
---

Completing Desert Treasure grants you access to **Ancient Magicks**, a powerful spellbook that is widely considered the best for magic-based PvM.

Ancient Magicks offers a variety of high-damage and area-of-effect spells, making it invaluable for both bossing and Slayer.

Unlocking Ancients also provides access to the **[Aspects of Vampyrism](https://runescape.wiki/w/Vampyrism)** and **[Penance](https://runescape.wiki/w/Penance)**, which offer early game lifesteal and prayer regeneration.
