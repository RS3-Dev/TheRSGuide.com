---
title: The Dig Site
description: Unlock powerful Tendrils abilities and progress towards Ancient Curses
---

Finishing the quest unlocks two powerful abilities: **Shadow Tendrils** and **Smoke Tendrils**. You'll also gain access to **Ice Asylum**, which is a niche healing ability.

The Dig Site is one of many prerequisites for unlocking **Ancient Curses**, which are critical for endgame PvM.
