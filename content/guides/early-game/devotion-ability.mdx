title: Devotion Ability
description: Unlock Devotion, Sacrifice, and Transfigure from One Piercing Note
---

One Piercing Note unlocks three abilities: **Devotion**, **Sacrifice**, and **Transfigure**.

Devotion in particular is one of the strongest defensive abilities in RuneScape, as it makes protection prayers and curses 100% effective for 10-20 seconds.
