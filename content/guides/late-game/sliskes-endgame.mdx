While the requirements list is daunting, Sliske's Endgame is one of the most rewarding quests in RuneScape.

Completing Sliske's Endgame grants you:
- 5x 250,000 experience lamps in the skill(s) of your choice which can be used on any skill over level 85
- The Ring of Whispers and Necklace of Shadows, which offer boosts to gathering skills.
