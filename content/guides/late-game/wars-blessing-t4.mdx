title: War's Blessing Tier 4
description: Unlock powerful rewards and bonuses by completing Combat Achievements
---

War's Blessing Tier 4 is a reward for reaching 1401 total combatscore, which can be gained by completing Combat Mastery Achievements.

## Rewards
It rewards the following benefits:
- Ability to choose the weekly rotation at Vorago, regardless of what week it is
- War's adrenaline crystals now increase to +100% per cycle instead of the usual +25%
- Guarenteed reaper's choice when getting a reaper assignment, for massive slayer experience and slayer points
- One-time reward of a costless Ancient Armour and Weapon Gizmo shell.
