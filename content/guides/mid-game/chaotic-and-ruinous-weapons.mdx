---
title: Chaotic and Ruinous Weapons
description: Efficient weapon upgrades from Dungeoneering rewards
---

Post-Dungeoneering remaster, Chaotics (tier 80) and Ruinous weapons (tier 90 with tier 100 accuracy) are extremely efficient upgrades. These can replace a traditional weapon upgrade path and take you directly to the endgame, as they are sufficient for completing almost any PvM content in RuneScape.

## Requirements

- Chaotics require 70 Dungeoneering
- Ruinous weapons require 90 Dungeoneering
  - Ironmen require 95 Dungeoneering for Ruinous weapons
