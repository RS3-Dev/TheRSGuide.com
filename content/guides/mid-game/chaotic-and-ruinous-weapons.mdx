---
title: Chaotic and Ruinous Weapons
description: High tier weapons that come from dungeoneering rewards
---

Post-Dungeoneering remaster, Chaotics (t80) and Ruinous weapons (t90 with t100 accuracy) are exteremely efficient upgrades. These can replace a traditional weapon upgrade path, and take you directly to the endgame, as they will be sufficient for allowing completetion of almost any PvM content in RuneScape.

## Requirements
- Chaotics require 70 Dungeoneering
- Ruinous weapons require 90 Dungeoneering
    - Ironmen require 95 Dungeoneering for Ruinous weapons
