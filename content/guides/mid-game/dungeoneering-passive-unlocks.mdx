Passive unlocks are powerful items or effects that provide ongoing benefits without requiring active use each time. They help automate repetitive tasks, boost experience gains, and improve overall efficiency in combat and skilling.

## Requirements

| Skill | Level |
|-------|-------|
| Dungeoneering | 70 |

## Key Passive Unlocks

### Charming Imp
This Dungeoneering reward can be stored in your inventory or pocket slot. It automatically collects summoning charms dropped by monsters, saving you significant time and inventory space during Slayer and combat training.

### Bonecrusher
Another Dungeoneering reward, the Bonecrusher automatically crushes bones dropped by monsters, granting you Prayer experience instantly. This is especially useful for Slayer tasks and general combat, as it passively trains Prayer without needing to pick up or bury bones manually.

### Ring of Vigour
Once unlocked, this ring can be worn or its effect can be permanently unlocked later in the game. It reduces the adrenaline cost of ultimate abilities by 10%, allowing for more frequent use of powerful attacks. This is a key upgrade for improving combat efficiency and is highly recommended for all combat styles.

### Gem Bag
The Gem bag holds up to 100 total uncut Sapphire, Emerald, Ruby or Diamonds and costs 2,000 Dungeoneering tokens. It can be upgraded further at 40 Dungeoneering and 45 Crafting to hold up to 60 of each of the previous tier gems as well as uncut Dragonstones.

### Smithing Autoheater
Uses coal from your ore back to automatically reheat unfinished smithing items as you smith them.

### Scroll of Life
Grants a passive 10% chance to save a seed when planting in a farming patch.

### Scroll of Cleansing
Grants a passive 10% chance to save a herblore secondary and make potions slightly faster

### Scroll of Efficiency
Grants a passive 2% chance to save a bar when smithing or grant an extra bar when smelting

### Scroll of Dexterity
Grants a passive chance to save crafting resources

### Scroll of Restoration
Grants a passive 2% chance to save materials when restoring an artifact
