---
title: Shadow's Grace
description: Archaeology relic that halves Surge, Escape, and Dive cooldowns
---

The Shadow's Grace relic allows you to surge, escape, and dive with half the usual cooldown

It's a massive mobility boost, although it does not stack with the Mobile invention perk.
