---
title: Unlocking the Vault of Hereditas
description: Heists, high-level Thieving, and tier 90 rewards
---

Leveling your thieving unlocks Heists which are a high level thieving activity with very powerful rewards, inluding the Gloomfire Bow (t90) and the Legatus Emberstaff (t90) both of which have powerful special attacks.

Having a high thieving level will grant access to significant AFK pickpocketing methods for upkeeping GP and resources including Clue Scrolls, Charms, Seeds, Onyxes, Pure Essence, Time Runes, and Binding contracts and the Am-Hej melee amulet.

## Requirements
Although the level requirement is 95, 102 Thieving is recommended for cracking the safes in the vault.
