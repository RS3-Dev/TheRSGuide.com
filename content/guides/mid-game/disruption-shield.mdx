---
title: Disruption Shield
description: Lunar spellbook shield ability
---

Disruption Shield is an extremely powerful defensive spell that can be used to negate a large amount of damage in PvM scenarios. It is unlocked from the Livid Farm shop for 90,000 Produce Points.
