---
title: The Gate of Elidinis
description: Skilling boss with powerful skilling rewards
---

The Gate of Elidinis provides very strong experience in Crafting, Divination, Mining, and Agility, as well as powerful skilling offhands; The Memory Dowser and the Runic Attuner, as well as the Scripture of Elidinis, which is the best skilling pocket slot item.

The Gate of Elidinis is a very strong unlock that is worth rushing, especially on an Ironman account
